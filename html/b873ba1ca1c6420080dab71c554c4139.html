Circular Dichroism Virtual Laboratory is design for mainly Under Graduate (3rd/4th year) and Post Graduate students. This lab will serve the purpose of interest of a large and diverse group of students.

Following students may be benefited from this lab.

  1. Undergraduate (B.Tech, B.Sc.), Postgraduates (M.Sc, M.Pharm) as part of courses Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Chemistry.
  2. Undergraduate and post graduate level (M.S., diploma, and Ph.D level) as part of courses Biotechnology, Biochemical engineering, Biophysics, Structural Biophysics, Structural Molecular Biology, Molecular Biophysics.

This lab may also be helpful for research scientists interested in photo-chemistry and photo-biology.
