2. Step-by-step Procedure

Step 1: Select any one of the two samples.
Step 2: Follow the instructions given in the Experimental-setup page.
Step 3: Select the temperature range and step size of your interest.
Step 4: Go through the instructions and fill up the table by clicking at the 222nm.
Step 5: Observe the plot and find out the transition temperature.
Step 6: Click on the “Measure Dy-node Voltage”.
Step 7: Follow the instruction and fill up the table by clicking at the 222nm.
Step 8: Observe the change in Dy-node voltage.
Step 9: Click on “Go to Analysis”
Step 10: Submit your answers. You can verify them from redoing the experiment or going through the theory.
Step 11: Once you have studied both the samples you may compare them by clicking “COMPARE”.
