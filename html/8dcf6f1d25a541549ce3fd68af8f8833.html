Basic understanding of high-school level science is sufficient to perform the experiments designed in this lab. But it is expected that a student will understand the following topics in details.

  1. Basic principles of Optics: light as an electromagnetic wave.
  2. Basic principles of Spectroscopy: a method to study atomic/molecular structure.
  3. Basic principles of Physical Chemistry: structure of an atom, inter atomic bonds, inter molecular bonds, ions, pH etc.
  4. Basic concepts of Biology: concept of cellular mechanisms, enzymes etc.

System Requirements

  1. The labs are best viewed in Google Chrome.
  2. Flash plugin must be installed in your browser.
  3. Java plugin must be installed in your browser.
