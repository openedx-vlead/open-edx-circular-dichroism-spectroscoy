Twin objectives:

I.

      To provide users with an understanding of
      • Basic concepts of chiroptical phenomenon
      • The scope and advantage of CD spectroscopy in studying biomolecular structure and dynamics

II.

    To provide virtual hands on experience in
    • Operating the CD Spectropolarimeter
    • Designing experiments for studying the stability of proteins under stress including, thermal, pH and various denaturant conditions

Larger goals:

    To enable users
  1. To work effectively with an actual CD Spectropolarimeter: From sample preparation to analysis
  2. To design and carry out CD experiments based on real life questions, particularly in the area of protein structure and dynamics
